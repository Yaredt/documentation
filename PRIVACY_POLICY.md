# MarketGuard Privacy Policy

**Last Updated**: January 27, 2025  
**Effective Date**: January 27, 2025

## Introduction

MarketGuard ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the MarketGuard Chrome extension ("Extension") and related services.

## Data Collection

### What Data We Collect

MarketGuard collects the following types of data:

1. **Conversation Messages**: 
   - Content of Facebook Messenger conversations related to Marketplace listings
   - Message timestamps and sender information
   - **Why**: Required to analyze buyer intent, detect potential scams, and generate lead scores

2. **User Preferences**:
   - Extension settings and configuration
   - Scoring preferences and thresholds
   - **Why**: To personalize your experience and remember your preferences

3. **Authentication Tokens**:
   - JWT tokens for backend API authentication
   - **Why**: To securely authenticate you with our backend services

4. **Usage Data**:
   - Feature usage statistics (which features you use)
   - Error logs and diagnostic information
   - **Why**: To improve the Extension and fix issues

### How We Collect Data

- **Directly from you**: When you use the Extension, it analyzes conversations visible in your Facebook Messenger interface
- **Automatically**: The Extension processes conversations as you view them in Messenger
- **From Facebook Messenger**: The Extension reads conversation content that is already visible to you in your browser

## Data Usage

### How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

1. **Lead Scoring**: Analyze buyer messages to provide lead scores and buying intent analysis
2. **Scam Detection**: Identify potential scam patterns and risk indicators in conversations
3. **Draft Reply Generation**: Generate suggested reply drafts based on conversation context
4. **Service Improvement**: Analyze usage patterns to improve Extension functionality
5. **Error Resolution**: Diagnose and fix technical issues

### Data Processing

- **Local Processing**: Some data processing occurs in your browser using the Extension
- **Backend Processing**: Conversation data is sent to our secure backend API for advanced analysis using machine learning and AI models
- **No Human Review**: Your conversations are not reviewed by human operators except for technical support purposes with your explicit consent

## Data Storage

### Where Data is Stored

1. **Chrome Local Storage**: 
   - User preferences and authentication tokens
   - Stored locally in your browser
   - Never transmitted to external servers

2. **Backend API**:
   - Conversation messages and analysis results
   - Stored securely on our servers
   - Encrypted in transit and at rest

3. **Retention Period**:
   - Conversation data: Retained for 90 days for analysis purposes
   - User preferences: Retained until you delete your account or uninstall the Extension
   - Authentication tokens: Stored locally until you log out

## Data Sharing

### We Do NOT Share Your Data

MarketGuard does not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties except:

1. **Service Providers**: We may use trusted service providers (hosting, analytics) who are contractually obligated to protect your data
2. **Legal Requirements**: We may disclose data if required by law or to protect our rights
3. **With Your Consent**: We will only share data with your explicit permission

### No Third-Party Advertising

- We do not use your data for advertising purposes
- We do not share data with advertising networks
- We do not track you across websites for advertising

## User Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

### Access

- You can view your stored preferences in the Extension settings
- You can request a copy of your data by contacting us

### Export

- You can export your conversation analysis data through the Extension
- Account data export is available through the Extension settings

### Deletion

- You can delete your account and all associated data at any time
- Uninstalling the Extension removes locally stored data
- Contact us to request deletion of backend-stored data
- Data deletion requests are processed within 30 days

### Correction

- You can update your preferences and settings at any time
- Contact us to correct any inaccurate data

## Security Measures

### How We Protect Your Data

1. **Encryption**:
   - All data transmitted to our backend API uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
   - Data at rest is encrypted using industry-standard encryption

2. **Access Controls**:
   - Backend systems use authentication and authorization controls
   - Only authorized personnel have access to data, and only for technical support purposes

3. **Secure Infrastructure**:
   - Our backend services are hosted on secure cloud infrastructure (Azure)
   - Regular security audits and updates

4. **Data Minimization**:
   - We only collect data necessary for Extension functionality
   - We do not collect unnecessary personal information
